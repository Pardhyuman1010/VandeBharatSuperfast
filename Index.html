About Vande Bharat Express

India's Engineering Marvel

Vande Bharat Express, also known as Train 18, is India's first indigenously designed and manufactured semi-high speed, self-propelled train set. It represents a major leap in Indian Railways' quest for speed and service.

The train was designed and built by the Integral Coach Factory (ICF) in Chennai in a record time of 18 months, showcasing India's engineering prowess.

Max Speed

180 km/h

Propulsion

Distributed Power System

Capacity

1,128 passengers

Launched

February 2019
